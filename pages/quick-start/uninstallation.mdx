# Uninstallation

To remove the Hivello Application from your Windows computer, follow these steps:

## Step 1

Go to the Settings page on your machine. Search for **Hivello** in the Apps > Installed apps

![Go to installed app Hivello](/images/uninstallation/go-to-installed-app-hivello.png)

## Step 2

Select the **three dots icon** and then select **Uninstall** option to uninstall the application.

![Uninstall application](/images/uninstallation/uninstall-application.png)

## Step 3

Wait for the application will be automatically uninstalled from your computer.
